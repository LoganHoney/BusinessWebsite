4 Point Inspection in Hernando County, FL

What Is a 4 Point Inspection?

A 4 point inspection focuses on the four major systems of a home: Roof, Electrical, Plumbing, and HVAC.

In Hernando County, insurance companies often require this inspection for homes over 20 years old before issuing or renewing coverage.

What Does It Include?

  • Roof age & condition
  • Electrical panel & wiring type
  • Plumbing materials & leaks
  • HVAC system age & performance

How Much Does a 4 Point Inspection Cost in Hernando County?

Pricing typically ranges from $75–$125 when bundled with a full home inspection. Standalone inspections may vary.

Do I Need One in Spring Hill or Brooksville?

Yes — most Florida insurance carriers require it for older homes in Spring Hill, Brooksville, Weeki Wachee, and surrounding areas.
