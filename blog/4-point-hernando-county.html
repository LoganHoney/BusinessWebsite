What Is a 4 Point Inspection?

A 4 Point Inspection evaluates the four most critical systems insurance companies look at before issuing coverage.

Roof

Age, condition, visible damage, and remaining life expectancy.

Electrical

Panel type, wiring material, grounding, and safety concerns.

Plumbing

Pipe materials, leaks, water heater age, and system condition.

HVAC

System age, operation, and overall functionality.

Why Do Insurance Companies Require It?

In Hernando County, most Florida insurance carriers require a 4 Point Inspection for homes over 20 years old.

This helps insurers evaluate risk related to aging roofs, outdated electrical panels, older plumbing systems, and HVAC performance.

Without this inspection, coverage may be denied or delayed.
